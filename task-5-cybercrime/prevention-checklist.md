##### Stay Safe Online

###### Actionable prevention tips relevant to college students in India:

1.Never enter UPI PIN to receive money

2.Do not scan UPI QR codes from unknown sources

3.Always verify links before clicking, like checking spelling, domain

4.Use strong, unique passwords for each account

5.Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities

6.Think before posting anything online

7.Do not trust unknown contacts on social media or messaging apps

8.Report suspicious accounts, links, or messages immediately

9.If you become a victim, immediately report to National Cyber Crime Portal: **cybercrime.gov.in** or their helpline: **1930** as soon as possible
