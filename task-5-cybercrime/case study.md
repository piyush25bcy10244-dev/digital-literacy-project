#### UPI/Online Payment Fraud

UPI is the short form for Unified Payements Interface, which is real time, instant payement system developed by National Payement Corporation of India (NPCI) that enables seamless money transfers between bank accounts via mobile apps.


###### What the crime is

UPI fraud is the misuse of a “collect request” feature. This feature was made to allow merchants set the amount they want to receive before the customer would pay. It shows the amount the merchant would receive on the UI. But, scammers use this feature to trick others by maniupulating them.


###### How it happens

Scammers first identify their target and then contact victims online (olx, whatsapp, Instagram) and pretend as buyer, seller or customer and promise that they are sending payemnet after gaining trust. But the game begins when he sends ‘collect request’ instead of the payment and the victim gets notification on app like Google pay or PhonePe. The message is confusing for them (request or receive). The scammer then manipulates them to enter their UPI pin to receive money, after which money gets debited instantly from vixtims account and the scammer disappears.



###### Who is targeted

Mostly students, new UPI users, people selling items online, less tech aware users, or elderly people get targeted in this scam.



###### Consequences

The consequences are that money is instantly debited, its difficult to recover money, emotional stress and panic.
