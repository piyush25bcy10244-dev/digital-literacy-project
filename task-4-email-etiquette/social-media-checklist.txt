Responsible Social media usage as a college student
5 Do's:
Always think before you post anything
Report and review suspicious activities immediately
Use strong password and privacy settings
Stay aware about online scams and avoid untrusted links
Never spend too much time, balance with studies
5 Don'ts:
Don't upload sensitive details about anyone
Don't use abusive or inappropriate language
Don't copy content without permission
Don't make fake accounts or try to scam someone
Don't spread negativity or hatred
